## Q2 2020
Launched [consbrgs.com](https://consbrgs.com/) during a 2am–5am on-prem cutover window, the week before I joined Northwestern Mutual. Consolidated Bearings is one of the nation's largest ball bearing suppliers — an early adopter of the web that digitized its catalog in the 90s. Begun as a Humble Humans effort in 2017, the project migrated a legacy .NET system to a modern CMS: 20k+ products and 55k taxonomy terms exported as raw data, restructured, validated, and ported over. When Humble Humans split from Merchwerks at the end of 2019, I honored the original contract and finished the swing. The site remains in my care today, slated for its next migration in 2027.
